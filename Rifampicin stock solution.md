# Rifampicin stock solution (50,000 ppm) or 1000X (50mg/ml)

1. Weigh 0.5g of Ampicillin

2. Dissolve in 10ml DMSO

***If using DMSO, do not filter (it will dissolve the filter itself)***
   
3. For 50ppm of media, use 500ul of the stock (adjust accordingly)

5. Store at -20C for future use (upto 1 year)
